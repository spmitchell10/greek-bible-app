Search Syntax Help

Basic Examples:

  • *λόγος@Nsg - Search entire NT (*) for λόγος as Noun (N), singular (s), genitive (g)
  • *εἰμί@VaAI3s - Search for εἰμί as Verb (V), aorist (a), Active (A), Indicative (I), 3rd person (3), singular (s)
  • *[article] & λόγος@Nsg - Article followed by λόγος (noun, singular, genitive)
  • *[article] & λόγος@Nsg W2 - Article within 2 words (W2) of λόγος
  • [Matt.] + [verb]@pAI3s - Search only in Matthew for present, active, indicative verbs
  • [Matt., Mk, Lk] + [verb]@aAI3s - Search in Matthew, Mark, and Luke for aorist verbs

Morphology Codes:

Part of Speech: N=noun, V=verb, J=adjective, D=adverb, C=conjunction, P=preposition, R=pronoun, T=particle, I=interjection
Case: n=nominative, g=genitive, d=dative, a=accusative, v=vocative
Number: s=singular, p=plural
Gender: m=masculine, f=feminine, u=neuter
Tense: p=present, i=imperfect, f=future, a=aorist, r=perfect, l=pluperfect
Voice: A=active, M=middle, P=passive
Mood: I=indicative, S=subjunctive, O=optative, M=imperative, N=infinitive, p=participle
Person: 1=1st, 2=2nd, 3=3rd

Special Tokens:

[article], [noun], [verb], [adjective], [pronoun], [preposition], [conjunction], [particle], [adverb]

Operators:

  • * - Search entire New Testament
  • [Book] + - Search specific book(s) (e.g., [Matt.] or [Matt., Mk, Lk])
  • @ - Specify morphology codes after a word
  • & - Join multiple search terms
  • W# - Words within # distance (e.g., W2 = within 2 words)
  • *rel - Relative search (similar vocabulary) e.g., *rel Rom. 8:1
  • *inf - Inference search (similar syntax) e.g., *inf Rom. 8:1

Book Abbreviations:

Matt, Mark, Mk, Luke, Lk, John, Jn, Acts, Rom, 1Cor, 2Cor, Gal, Eph, Phil, Col, 1Thess, 2Thess, 1Tim, 2Tim, Titus, Phlm, Heb, Jas, 1Pet, 2Pet, 1John, 2John, 3John, Jude, Rev

Relative Search (Similar Vocabulary):

Use *rel or rel followed by a verse reference to find verses with similar vocabulary (parallel passages).

  • *rel Rom. 8:1 - Find verses with similar words to Romans 8:1
  • rel John 3:16 - Find verses with similar words to John 3:16
  • *rel Matt. 6:9 - Find parallels to the Lord's Prayer
  • After searching, you can refine by selecting specific words to include
  • Matching words are shown in bold

Inference Search (Similar Syntax):

Use *inf or inf followed by a verse reference to find verses with similar grammatical structure.

  • *inf Rom. 8:1 - Find verses with similar syntax to Romans 8:1
  • inf John 1:1 - Find verses with similar structure to John 1:1
  • *inf Matt. 5:3 - Find verses structured like the first Beatitude
  • Results show similarity percentage (0-100%)
  • Words and patterns are color-coded by part of speech
  • Each POS has a unique color: nouns (blue), verbs (red), adjectives (green), etc.
